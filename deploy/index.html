I'm based in Wellington, NZ and have over two years experience working on immersive web and mobile experiences for local industry force Resn, working in teams that have won FWA's, Pixel Awards, Best Awards, Webby Awards, Awwwards, and more along the way.

I've worked on projects for Google, Toyota, Hennessy, FedEx, EDF, Budweiser, and Carls Jr., as well as locals RNZAF, Lucille, Pacific Screen, Forest and Bird, and Smoking Not Our Future. You'll see a selection of them in the work section.

I'm now working freelance to help local businesses get themselves online, and as a means to pursue my own creative interests and collaborate with like-minded people on exciting new projects.

Websites are my specialty but I can also help with smartphone apps and branding too.

Technically, I use HTML5, CSS3, jQuery, and Javascript to build my websites, utilising libraries like Handlebars and require.js, and preprocessors like SASS (or LESS), to make the dev process even more efficient.
